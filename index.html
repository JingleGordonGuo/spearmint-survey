Meet the Spear
Read the description below carefully before answering.
🗡️ What is the Spear?
The Spear is a wellbeing device designed to hijack the smoking habit loop — without nicotine, tobacco, or harmful chemicals.
🌿It looks and feels exactly like a cigarette. Inside is crushed aromatic tea leaves sealed in a breathable mesh. Every slow draw delivers a clean, flavoured breath — like a moment of meditation.
☕When you're done, you can tear it open and steep the contents in hot water — turning your break into a warm drink with the full benefits of the tea leaves.
🛡️It comes with a ring worn on your smoking finger — engraved with your family surname — to remind you who you're fighting for every time you reach for a cigarette.
🤝Friends and family can sponsor your quit journey. Non-smokers get their own Spear too — joining the break, joining the movement.
♻️Fully biodegradable. Unlike cigarette butts, every Spear composts completely.
