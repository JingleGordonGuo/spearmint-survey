Meet the Spear
Read the description below carefully before answering.
What is the Spear?
The Spear is a cigarette-shaped device designed as a non-nicotine alternative to smoking. It contains no nicotine, tobacco, or combustion.
🌿It is shaped like a cigarette and contains crushed aromatic tea leaves in a breathable mesh. Drawing on it delivers a flavoured breath without smoke or vapour.
🍵After use, the tea leaves inside can be steeped in hot water to make a drink.
🛡️It comes with a ring worn on your smoking finger — engraved with your family surname — as a personal reminder during cravings.
🤝Friends and family can sponsor a smoker's quit journey. Non-smokers can also receive their own Spear to use during shared breaks.
♻️The Spear is fully biodegradable and compostable.
